DMRE Certified Licensed Petroleum Operator

Pee Zet Petroleum & Trading (Pty) Ltd is a fully licensed petroleum business providing dependable fuel and energy solutions to Umzimkhulu and surrounding KwaZulu-Natal communities.

We serve individual motorists, commercial operators, agricultural businesses, and institutional clients with a comprehensive product range — from everyday regulated pump grades to specialised aviation fuels and LPG. Our operations are anchored in safety compliance, product integrity, and genuine community investment.
